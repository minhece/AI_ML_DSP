My Name is Minh Nguyen, and I love playing Guitar

I am a PhD student at Johannes Kepler University Linz and guitar becomes a part of my life. I think music is the best way to connect people. Therefore, I want to teach guitar and ukulele in Linz. I played guitar and ukulele for 10 years. I also teached guitar and performed in my home country Vietnam and in the United States of America (I studied my master in the U.S). I want to bring the easiest and comfortable way to study guitar for my students.

Teaching

Fingerstyle guitar and ukulele

The goal of my teaching method is to help the students can self-study guitar. The first beginner may feel don't know what to do with the guitar and they can make mistakes and then these mistakes become a bad habit for playing guitar. With my teaching method, the student can self-study after 2 hours and they know exactly what to do in the future.

I play fingerstyle guitar. So, Fingerstyle guitar is the technique of playing the guitar or bass guitar by plucking the strings directly with the fingertips, fingernails, or picks attached to fingers, as opposed to flatpicking.

Guarantee

First hour

The student will know exactly what to do with the guitar. They will learn some typical mistake for playing guitar or ukulele and how to fix this.

The student will know how to read guitar tab without music theory. No music theory for playing guitar. Of course, music theory is the good way to improve playing guitar. But for beginner, they don't need to know anything about music theory and they still can play guitar or ukulele.

The student can play a simple song.

The student will practice for a week before learning second hour.

Second hour

The student will learn basic chords and combine with simple song to play a complex song. Of course, the student need to practice for another week.

Third hour

The student will learn how to combine chords while singing a song. After the third hour, the student can study guitar and develope more by themself.
